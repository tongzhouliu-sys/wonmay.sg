Our Story

20 Years of
Digital Heritage.
Global Ambition.

From a technology company founded in Wuhan in 2007, to a Singapore-headquartered global cloud and AI infrastructure group — RHCLOUD's journey reflects the evolution of China's tech sector onto the world stage.

Group Structure

Global HQ.
Local Expertise.

RHCLOUD PTE. LTD. was established in Singapore as the group's global decision-making headquarters. We operate on a model of "centralized group resources, independently operated businesses" — combining the financial and technology resources of the group with the agility of regionally-focused business units.

Our anchor operating member, 武汉完美网络科技有限公司 (Wuhan Perfect Network Technology), has served as the group's technical foundation for nearly two decades — earning recognition as a National High-Tech Enterprise and building deep expertise in cloud infrastructure and managed services.

  • Wuhan R&D — Engineering, platform development, China ops

Our Journey

Two Decades of Growth

2007
Foundation

武汉完美网络科技有限公司 is founded in Wuhan, China, beginning its journey as an internet infrastructure and managed services provider.

2012
Cloud Era Begins

Early adoption of cloud computing services. The company becomes an authorized reseller for China's emerging cloud platforms and builds its first enterprise client base.

2016
National High-Tech Recognition

Officially recognized as a National High-Tech Enterprise (国家高新技术企业) by the Chinese government — validating the company's R&D capability and technical excellence.

2020
Alibaba Cloud Strategic Partnership

Elevated to Alibaba Cloud Strategic Partner status, unlocking deep cooperation on enterprise deals, co-marketing, and preferred pricing structures.

2022
AI Model Platform Launch

Launched the aggregate AI model platform, providing enterprises with unified access to global LLMs through a single API gateway — ahead of the mainstream AI adoption wave.

2024
Global Expansion

RHCLOUD PTE. LTD. incorporated in Singapore as the group's global headquarters. Expansion into Hong Kong and the United States markets begins.

2025
MSP Platform Rollout

Launched 行云管家 multi-cloud management platform, delivering unified hybrid cloud operations for enterprise clients across Southeast Asia and Greater China.

2026 →
The AI-Native Era

Transitioning from cloud infrastructure to AI-native intelligence partner — helping enterprises adopt and operationalize frontier AI models across their businesses.
